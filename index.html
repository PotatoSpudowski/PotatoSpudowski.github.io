About Me

He/Him

Hey there! My name is Bahushruth and I am an undergrad student. I am currently a "GRM Student" at IBM Research - India working on "Multimodality and Information Retrieval" advised by Seema Nagar ma'am. Previously I was at Entrepreneur First as a "Founder in Residence", where I worked on NLP solutions to help the Tier 2 and 3 kids achieve proficiency in answering open domain subjective exam questions. Prior to EF I was a "GRM Student" at IBM Research - India where I worked on "Modelling Diffusion and Propagation of Affect in Social Networks" advised by Seema Nagar ma'am and Dr. Kuntal Dey sir.

My interests lie in NLP, Multi-modal research, Graph ML and building technology to solve the problems for the Tier 2 and Tier 3 communities. For more information about me please check out my updated CV.

I am always looking for Graduate positions or Research Opportunities. If my work and interests align with your work, and if you have open positions, please feel free to contact me.
