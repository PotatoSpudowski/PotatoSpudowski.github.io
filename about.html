About

I'm Bahushruth CS, currently building AI for lending at Cassian, where we're automating borrower onboarding in commercial lending. We're taking messy, incomplete borrower submissions and turning them into clean, decision-ready loan packages for brokers and lenders.

Previously, I was part of Entrepreneurs First (Fall '25), where I explored vertical AI solutions before co-founding Cassian. Before that, I worked as an MLOps Engineer at QuillBot—first MLOps hire to maintain GPU infrastructure and deploy ML systems serving millions of users daily. I also spent time as a Founding Engineer at Flippy (YC-backed crypto startup) and as a Research Intern at IBM Research working on multimodal information retrieval.

My background spans machine learning infrastructure, NLP, and graph neural networks. I'm particularly interested in efficient LLM fine-tuning, retrieval-augmented generation, and productionizing AI systems that solve real business problems.
